National Poster Competition

Deadline 24 August, 2018

{{ partial "svg/hero-poster.svg" . }}

Young & Powerful is a poster design competition, inspired by Graphic Design Festival Scotland. It is a celebration of youth, their ambition, creativity and desire to make this world a better place, for a better future.

Open to all ages between 12–24, Young & Powerful is a platform that will give a the young a visual voice, to inspire hope through the eyes of the youth in Singapore.

Selected entries will be displayed at National Museum of Singapore on 1 October to the public. The best poster in each of the three different age groups will be picked and offered a $1,000 cash prize. The posters will be also displayed in select MRT stations in Singapore.

Submission

Submit online or by post.

Online
Upload My Design Upload 1 poster per submission.
By Post

Please provide the following information if you are submitting by post.

  • Full name
  • Age
  • Email Address
  • Mobile Number
  • School / College / University (if applicable)
  • Workplace / Community Group / Organization (if applicable)

Entries without these details won’t be considered for judging.

Please remember to package it securely. Mailing tubes are recommended.

{{ partial "svg/mailing-tube.svg" . }}

Mailing tubes are available at the post office.

Judging

Independent panel of judges will review all entries and 60 selected ones – 20 under each age group.

The best entry in each age group will also win a cash prize of $1,000.
