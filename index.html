Young & Powerful

National Poster Competition

Deadline 24 August, 2018

Young & Powerful is a poster design contest, inspired by Graphic Design Festival Scotland(link). It is a celebration of youth, their ambition, creativity and desire to make this world a better place, for a better future.

Open to all ages between 12â€“24, Young & Powerful is a platform that will give a the young a visual voice, to inspire hope through the eyes of the youth in Singapore.

Selected entries will be displayed to the public on October 1, 2018 at National Museum of Singapore. The posters will be also displayed in select MRT stations in Singapore. The best poster in each of the three different age groups will be offered a $1,000 cash prize.

Project Topics

Create posters based on any one of the topics below.

Equality

Examples for inspiration: Equal rights, celebrate diversity, end discrimination, gender equality.

Democracy

Examples for inspiration: Voice for all, exercise to vote, power of active citizenship.

Get active, stay healthy

Examples for inspiration: Healthy eating, exercise and sports, the importance of mental health, staying active.

Stay hopeful, be positive

Examples for inspiration: Optimism, positive attitude, creative thinking.

Format

Create posters based on these specifications and rules.

Posters must be A3 in size, landscape or portrait.

Posters can be created by hand or digitally.

No limit on the number of poster entries.

Submit your posters independently or as a group.

Submission

Submit online or by post.

Upload My Design Upload 1 poster per submission.
